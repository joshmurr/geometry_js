This is an experiment in implementing parametric formulae in Javascript as a means to help me understand the process of generating a 3D mesh.

The render process is very simple: the points are drawn to the canvas as lines which are scaled by their z-coordinate; the vectors are all 3D vectors; there are no matrix transformations.

This is pretty much at the limit of what can be done without re-building the 3D engine into something more sophisticated.
